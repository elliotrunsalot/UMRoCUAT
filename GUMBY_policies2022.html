Entry Discounts

AURA members and UMROC members receive a 10% discount on all entry types (discounts are not cumulative).

Waitlist

For those that miss out an entry, a waitlist will be established. The price of your entry will be determined by the date you were added to the waitlist. For example, if you registered in October during the early bird period, but you were not offered an entry until March, you would still pay the early bird price.

Withdrawals

If you cannot participate in the event for any reason, entry fee refunds are available in accordance with the below-stated withdrawal dates only. After the stipulated dates, entry fees are non-refundable for withdrawals initiated by the athlete.Requests for refunds must be notified to the organiser via email info@ultramediocre.run

Withdrawal Refunds

Where eligible, please allow up to 30 business days for your refund to be processed. The refund is automatically issued to the credit card originally used to pay for the registration. If this card has since been cancelled or has expired it is the athlete’s responsibility to notify us when requesting the eligible refund.

  • Prior to 30 November 2021, 5pm — 75% of entry fee refundable for athlete withdrawal requests made up to this date.
  • Prior to 31 of January 2022, 5pm — 50% of entry fee refundable for athlete withdrawal requests made up to this date.
  • Prior to 23 February 2022, 5pm — 25% of entry fee refundable for athlete withdrawal requests made up to this date. No refunds after this date.

Transfer of entry to different race

Transfers between GUMBY and GUMBaby races can be requested up until such time as that event distance is sold out, subject to the following terms:

  • To request a transfer from one event to another, you must email us at info@ultramediocre.run on or before the close of entries (11:59pm Friday 8 April 2022)
  • Where you request to transfer to the longer event (GUMBaby to the GUMBY), you are required to pay the difference in entry fees between the two distances (as per the applicable entry fees on your original day of entry).
  • Where you request to transfer to the shorter event (GUMBY to the GUMBaby) the difference in entry fees will not be refunded.

Transfer of entry to another runner

Transfers will be available at no additional cost until the close of entries.

  • To request a transfer from one person to another, you must email us at info@ultramediocre.run on or before the close of entries (11:59pm Friday 8 April 2022).
  • Any request to transfer entries must include the name and contact details of the person taking your entry.
  • Upon receipt of your transfer request you will be withdrawn from the event and a registration code will be issued to the person taking your entry.
  • Any payment for the entry is to be made between the parties involved in the transfer.

COVID-19 Policy

If the event is cancelled due to COVID-19 or some other reason beyond the control of the organisers, entrants will be offered the opportunity to participate in a rescheduled event or transfer entries to the following year.

If the entrant does not wish to participate in a rescheduled event or transfer entries to the following year, we offer a refund in line with the refund policy (if cancelled before 23 Feb) or a credit for future ULTRA Mediocre events (valid for two years from the original event date).

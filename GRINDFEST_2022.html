Run, walk or crawl for 6hrs or 12hrs, solo or in a team. The 2021 inaugural GRINDFEST will take place Lennox Gardens. The event promises a vibrant community atmosphere for runners and supporters in the environs of Nara Peace Park and the Beijing Gardens with views across Lake Burley Griffin to Black Mountain and the Brindabellas beyond.

The races are organised by ULTRA Mediocre Runners of Canberra Incorporated, a not for profit sports club furthering the sport of ultrarunning in Canberra and surrounds.

The Races

GRINDFEST is a timed 6hr/12hr race on a 2km course. The participant or team with greatest distance in their category wins.

When

October 24 2021. Races start at 8am.

Where

Lennox Gardens, Flynn Drive, Yarralumla ACT.

The Course

The course is 2kms of bituman, except for a short stretch of grass near Commonwealth Avenue Bridge. Participants with travel in a counter-clockwise direction.

There will be places for tables and support crew to offer aid near the lap start/finish line on the lake side of the gardens

Public toilets and portaloos for participants use are available on the Flynn Drive side.

The Rules

Runners run the same course in the same direction until the time is up

Solo

Teams

Only 1 runner from each team may be on the course at any given time. They can decide how many laps to run before changing.

Race HQ

We encourage participants you take your own rubbish home with you, but there are also rubbish bins near the public toilets.

There are taps and water fountains in the Gardens.

Aid Station

Runners and teams are to make their own arrangements for refreshments, which they may set up in a small area at the start/finish line.
